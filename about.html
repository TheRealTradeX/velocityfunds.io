A Quant-Driven Prop Firm for Traders Who Think in Systems.

Velocity Funds is a modern futures prop firm engineered for traders who see markets through structure, probability, and algorithmic behavior — not emotion or hype. We’re building more than evaluations. We’re building the foundation for a future quant desk powered by trader talent.

Velocity Funds was created with a simple belief: the next generation of elite traders won’t come from Wall Street recruiting funnels — they’ll come from independent thinkers who understand liquidity, market microstructure, and the logic beneath price movement.

Markets are not random. They are algorithmic, repeatable, and heavily influenced by predictable liquidity events. Our role is to build an environment where disciplined traders can express that edge with clarity, structure, and scalable buying power.

We model ourselves after firms like Jane Street, Jump Trading, DRW, Hudson River — not with noise, gimmicks, or marketing tricks, but with data, risk modeling, and systems-first thinking.
